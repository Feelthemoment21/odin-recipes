Bibimbap Recipes

Description:

A colorful and healthy Korean dish made of warm rice, assorted vegetables,
protein (usually beef or egg), and gochujang (Korean chili paste), all mixed together right before eating.

Ingredients:

  • 2 bowls of cooked white rice (short- or medium-grain is best)
  • 200g (7 oz) ground or sliced beef (ribeye or sirloin)
  • Carrot, zucchini
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup spinach
  • Some kimchi
  • 1 cup of bean sprouts
  • 2 tbsp gochujang
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp rice vinegar or water and some minced garlic

Steps:

  1. Cook and prep the vegetables
  2. Cook the beef with soy sauce, sugar, sesame oil, garlic and pepper
  3. Mix all the sauce ingredients in a small bowl
  4. Fry eggs sunny side up so the yolk stays runny
  5. Add every thing together and mix thoroughly so the sauce coats all the ingredients
