Pho Recipes

Description:

Experience the magic of one of the greatest noodle soups in the world with this easy to follow traditional Vietnamese Pho recipe!
Made from scratch with the signature broth that’s light yet at the same time so full of flavour, it’s infused with spices like cinnamon, star anise and cardamom.
The soup is utterly addictive and every spoonful leaves you wanting more!

Ingredients:

  • Beef bone broth
  • Pho herbal and spices
  • Thin raw beef slices
  • Hoisin sauce
  • Sriracha sauce
  • Green onions

Steps:

  1. Sim mer the beef bone and beef tender
  2. Boil the noodles
  3. Add the Pho rice noodles in the bowl, add the broth and beef tender as well as some raw thin slices of beef sirloin.
  4. Add some green onions on top the bowl and add some sriracha and hoisin sauce also to add more flavour to the bowl and enjoy!
