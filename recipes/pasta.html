Pasta Recipes

Description:

This easy pasta dish features tender spaghetti noodles topped with a hearty, flavorful sauce made from ground beef and canned tomato sauce.
The ground beef is browned and seasoned, then simmered with the tomato sauce to create a rich, savory meat sauce.
Served hot over the noodles, itâ€™s a comforting and satisfying meal perfect for busy weeknights.

Ingredients:

  • 200g (about half a box) spaghetti noodles
  • 250g (about 1/2 lb) ground beef
  • 1 can (about 400g/14 oz) tomato sauce
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced (optional)
  • 1 small onion, diced (optional)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil or cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 tsp dried Italian herbs or oregano (optional)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional, for topping)

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti
  2. Cook the ground beef
  3. Add the tomato sauce
  4. Combine and serve the meat sauce over the cooked spaghetti.
    Top with grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
